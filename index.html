About the Artist

Hi, I'm Irya — the hands and heart behind Studio Kher. My journey with art began when I was just four years old, and it has been a passionate part of my life ever since. Over the years, I’ve explored various mediums including shading, painting, colour pencil, charcoal, oil pastel, and soft pastel. Every piece I create is handmade with care and a love for detail.
